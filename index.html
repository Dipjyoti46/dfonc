Overview

It pervades all spheres of life, and has been around as long as life has taken up the task of purposefully changing the world around it. Research into design and the emergence of a research community in this area has been relatively new. Its development has been influenced by the multiple facets of design (human, artefact, process, organisation, the micro- and macro economy and the ecology by which design is shaped) and the associated diversification of the community depending on the facets of focus or that of their applications. Design is complex, balancing the needs of multiple stakeholders, and requiring a multitude of areas of knowledge to be utilised, and resources spread across space and time.

Design meets
Technology and
Business.

INDIS is the eleventh in a series of conferences intended to be held every two years in India to bring together the international community from diverse areas of design practice, education and research. Explore the dynamic world of design research at ICoRD'27 from January 6th to 9th, 2027, at the Department of Design and Manufacturing, Indian Institute of Science, Bengaluru. Call for Paper / Projects

With peer-reviewed publications, visual papers, workshops, and keynote sessions, INDIS 2026 fosters conversations that move from Indian contexts to global perspectives, positioning design as a strategic and transformative force.

Join us to reflect, research, and re imagine design led innovation by connecting local insights with global discourse across design, technology, and business.
