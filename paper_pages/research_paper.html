Full Research Papers

Full Research Papers present completed research with clear theoretical or empirical contributions to design and innovation studies. Submissions should demonstrate methodological rigor and clearly articulate research questions, methods, and findings.

Typical Structure

  1. Title and Abstract (150–250 words)
  2. Keywords (4–6)
  3. Introduction and Research Problem
  4. Literature Review / Theoretical Background
  5. Research Methodology
  6. Results / Findings
  7. Discussion
  8. Contributions to Design and Innovation Studies
  9. Limitations and Future Work
  10. References

Length

  1. Maximum 6 pages including references
  2. Papers exceeding the page limit will be desk rejected.

Paper Submission

INDIS 2026 - International Conference on Design & Innovation Studies

All submissions to INDIS 2026 – International Conference on Design and Innovation Studies must be made electronically through the EasyChair conference management system.

Authors must submit manuscripts in PDF format via the official submission portal.


Submission Process

  1. Create or log in to an EasyChair account.
  2. Select the appropriate submission category
    • Full Research Paper
    • Case Study and Industry Paper
    • Research-through-Design Contribution
    • Poster / Short Paper
  3. Enter the required metadata
    • Paper title
    • Abstract
    • Keywords
    • Author affiliations
  4. Upload manuscript PDF
  5. Confirm submission

Submission Requirements

  • Papers must be written in English
  • Manuscripts must be in PDF format
  • Maximum 6 pages including references
  • Submissions must be original and unpublished
  • No simultaneous submissions allowed

Review Process

All submissions undergo peer review.

  • Relevance to conference themes
  • Originality
  • Methodological rigor
  • Clarity of presentation
  • Contribution to the field

Anonymization

  • Remove author names
  • Remove affiliations
  • Anonymize self citations
  • Remove document metadata

INDIS Publication

INDIS 2026 - International Conference on Design & Innovation Studies

Accepted papers will be published in the Springer conference proceedings subject to publisher formatting requirements.

Selected high-quality papers may be invited for extended journal submission.


Important Notes

  • Only registered and presented papers will be published
  • Papers must follow Springer formatting
  • Journal invitations will undergo independent review
