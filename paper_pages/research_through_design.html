Research-through-Design Contributions

Research-through-Design (RtD) Contributions present knowledge generated through the act of designing. Submissions must demonstrate how design activity produced generalizable knowledge, not only artifacts or design outcomes.

Note: Pure design portfolios without research reflection will not be accepted.

Typical Structure

  1. Title and Abstract (150–250 words)
  2. Keywords (4–6)
  3. Research Question or Design Inquiry
  4. Background and Related Work
  5. Design Process
  6. Artifacts or Prototypes
  7. Reflection and Knowledge Contribution
  8. Discussion
  9. Limitations
  10. References

Length & Requirements

  • Maximum 6 pages including references
  • Must clearly articulate knowledge contribution
  • Must demonstrate how the design process generated knowledge
  • Must explain why this knowledge matters beyond the project

Important Requirements

  1. What knowledge was produced – Articulate the insights, principles, or understanding generated.
  2. How the design process generated knowledge – Explain the design methodology and reflection process.
  3. Why this knowledge matters beyond the project – Demonstrate broader applicability and contribution.

Paper Submission

INDIS 2026 - International Conference on Design & Innovation Studies

All submissions to INDIS 2026 – International Conference on Design and Innovation Studies must be made electronically through the EasyChair conference management system.

Authors must submit manuscripts in PDF format via the official submission portal.


Submission Process

  1. Create or log in to an EasyChair account.
  2. Select the appropriate submission category
    • Full Research Paper
    • Case Study and Industry Paper
    • Research-through-Design Contribution
    • Poster / Short Paper
  3. Enter the required metadata
    • Paper title
    • Abstract
    • Keywords
    • Author details and affiliations
  4. Upload the manuscript in PDF format.
  5. Include visual documentation of design artifacts.
  6. Confirm and finalize the submission.

Authors may update their submissions any time before the submission deadline.


Submission Requirements

  • Papers must be written in English
  • Manuscripts must be submitted in PDF format
  • Maximum 6 pages including references
  • Visual documentation of design artifacts
  • Knowledge contribution statement
  • Submissions must be original and unpublished
  • Simultaneous submission to other conferences or journals is not permitted

Review Process

All submissions will undergo peer review by members of the Technical Program Committee and external reviewers.

  • Clarity of research question or design inquiry
  • Rigor of design process and documentation
  • Quality of reflection and knowledge articulation
  • Contribution to design research methodology
  • Generalizability beyond the specific project

Anonymization

  • Author names and affiliations are removed from the manuscript
  • Self-references are anonymized where necessary
  • Document properties do not reveal author identity
  • Visual materials should not contain identifying information

INDIS Publication

INDIS 2026 - International Conference on Design & Innovation Studies

All accepted and presented papers will be published in the Springer conference proceedings subject to publisher formatting requirements.

Selected high-quality papers may be invited for extended journal submission.


Important Notes

  • Only registered and presented papers will be included in the proceedings
  • Papers must comply with Springer formatting requirements
  • Invited journal submissions will undergo independent peer review
  • Conference acceptance does not guarantee journal publication
