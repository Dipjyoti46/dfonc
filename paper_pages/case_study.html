Case Studies and Industry Papers

INDIS 2026 - International Conference on Design & Innovation Studies

Case Studies and Industry Papers present documented real-world design or innovation projects from academia, industry, government, or non-profit sectors. Submissions should focus on practical insights and lessons learned, rather than theoretical development.

Note: Pure marketing or product descriptions will not be accepted.

Typical Structure

  1. Title and Abstract (150–250 words)
  2. Keywords (4–6)
  3. Context and Problem Description
  4. Stakeholders and Constraints
  5. Approach and Process
  6. Methods or Tools Used
  7. Implementation
  8. Outcomes and Evaluation
  9. Lessons Learned
  10. Implications for Practice
  11. References (if applicable)

Length

  1. Maximum 6 pages including references

Paper Submission

INDIS 2026 - International Conference on Design & Innovation Studies

All submissions to INDIS 2026 – International Conference on Design and Innovation Studies must be made electronically through the EasyChair conference management system.

Authors are required to submit their manuscripts in PDF format through the official submission portal. Submissions sent by email or other means will not be considered.

The EasyChair submission link will be provided on this page.

Submission Process

Authors should follow the steps below to submit their paper:

  1. Create or log in to an EasyChair account.
  2. Select the appropriate submission category:
    • Full Research Paper
    • Case Study and Industry Paper
    • Research-through-Design Contribution
    • Poster / Short Paper
  3. Enter the required metadata:
    • Paper title
    • Abstract
    • Keywords
    • Author details and affiliations
  4. Upload the manuscript in PDF format.
  5. Confirm and finalize the submission.

Authors may update their submissions any time before the submission deadline.

Submission Requirements

  • Papers must be written in English
  • Manuscripts must be submitted in PDF format
  • Submissions must follow the INDIS 2026 formatting guidelines
  • Submissions must comply with the specified page limits
  • Submissions must be original and unpublished
  • Simultaneous submission to other conferences or journals is not permitted

Review Process

All submissions will undergo peer review by members of the Technical Program Committee and external reviewers.

Review criteria include:

  • Relevance to the conference themes
  • Practical value and applicability
  • Quality of documentation and evaluation
  • Clarity of presentation
  • Lessons learned and insights provided

Important Note on Anonymization

Submissions should be prepared for double-blind review.

Authors should ensure that:

  • Author names and affiliations are removed from the manuscript
  • Organization names should be anonymized (e.g., "Company X")
  • Document properties do not reveal author identity
  • Self-references are appropriately anonymized

Detailed instructions for preparing anonymized submissions will be provided.

INDIS Publication

INDIS 2026 - International Conference on Design & Innovation Studies

All accepted and presented papers at INDIS 2026 – International Conference on Design and Innovation Studies will be published in the conference proceedings by Springer, subject to compliance with the publisher's submission and formatting requirements.

Authors of accepted papers will be required to revise their manuscripts in accordance with the Springer proceedings format and submission guidelines, which will be provided at a later stage on the conference website.

In addition to the proceedings, a limited number of high-quality papers will be shortlisted by the Technical Program Committee and invited for extended submission to an international peer-reviewed journal. Details of the journal and submission process will be announced separately.

Important Notes

  • Only registered and presented papers will be included in the Springer proceedings
  • Papers must comply with the Springer formatting requirements (to be updated)
  • Invited journal submissions will undergo independent peer review according to the journal's policies
  • Acceptance in the conference does not guarantee journal publication
